Stud Earring
Design: Simple and elegant, with a single stone or ornament attached directly to the earlobe.
Type: Diamond studs.
Occasions: Perfect for daily wear and formal occasions.
Fun Fact: Stud earrings are one of the most ancient forms of jewelry, dating back to at least 1500 B.C.

Huggie Earring
Design: Small, thick hoops that "hug" the earlobe.
Comfort: Known for being comfortable to wear and secure.
Styles: Often adorned with diamonds, gemstones, or intricate designs.
Fun Fact: Huggie earrings are a modern trend that has gained popularity for their chic and minimalist look.

Hoop Earring
Design: Circular or semi-circular earrings that loop through the ear.
Sizes: Come in various sizes, from small and delicate to large and bold.
Styles: Plain hoops, embellished with stones or charms, and huggie hoops (smaller and closer to the earlobe).
Fun Fact: Hoop earrings have been a staple in many cultures, including ancient Egypt and Rome.

Dangle Earring
Design: Earrings that hang down from the earlobe, often featuring intricate designs.
Lengths: Range from short drops to shoulder-length dangles.
Variety: Can be simple chains, elaborate chandeliers, or artistic pieces.
Fun Fact: Dangle earrings can add a touch of drama and elegance to any outfit, making them popular for evening wear.

Ear Cuff Earring
Design: Earrings that wrap around or cling to the outer edge of the ear, no piercing required in many cases.
Variety: Available in sleek minimalist bands, ornate designs with gemstones, or even edgy styles featuring spikes or chains.
Versatility: Can be worn solo for a bold statement or paired with other earrings for a layered look.
Fun Fact: Ear cuffs date back to ancient times, with evidence of them being used in various cultures like ancient Egypt and India to make unique fashion statements.
